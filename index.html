06

Questions,
answered.

Do I need to write any content or train it myself?

No. You give us your website URL and we do the rest — Ansa reads your existing pages and builds the knowledge base automatically. If something's missing from your site, you can add a few notes, but most businesses are live without writing a word.

Will it make up prices or promises?

Ansa answers from your own pages only. When it doesn't know something, it says so and offers to take the customer's details so you can follow up — instead of guessing. That's the difference between a receptionist and a generic chatbot.

How do I get the leads?

However you want them: an instant email, a text to your phone, and a row in a Google Sheet — all at once, the second a lead is captured. On higher plans we can push them to your CRM or any tool via webhook.

What does it take to install?

One line of code pasted before the closing </body> tag — or we install it for you for free. It works on WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, GoDaddy, Shopify and custom sites.

Can it book appointments?

Yes. Out of the box it collects preferred days and times with the lead. On Pro and up it connects to Cal.com so customers can pick a real slot right inside the chat.

What if my website doesn't have everything?

That's common. During setup you can add extra facts — pricing, FAQs, service details — and Ansa uses those alongside your pages. If your site is genuinely thin, we also offer a website tune-up / landing page add-on so there's more for visitors to convert on.

Is it secure? Can it be misused?

Yes. The assistant is locked to your business — it stays on topic, refuses off-topic or trick prompts, and never exposes any keys or internal data (those live on a secure server, never in the website). It's safe to run on any site, including static hosts.

How fast can I be live?

Usually within a day. We point Ansa at your site, tune the greeting, send you the snippet, and you're capturing leads the same afternoon.
